About Me

I’m a French engineer. Voilà.

I am interested in human-centered initiatives that can improve day-to-day life. I believe in a technology that fits and adapts to our needs, as we unfortunately too often observe individuals adapting themself to technology. With a strong emphasis on the healthcare field, most of my projects consist in investigating anthropometrics - whether to help physicians make decision or patients move their bodies, but over all, to blink a LED. You can find some of them here on this website.

Reality is I spend most of my time drinking coffee.

My background lies in hardware systems engineering. My professional experiences mostly relate to the medical industry and include topics such as bio-sensing techniques, clinical decision support systems or assistive technologies.
